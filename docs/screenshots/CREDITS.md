# Screenshot credits

The photos shown in the Cullimingo screenshots are **placeholders**, not part of
the app. They are served by [Lorem Picsum](https://picsum.photos/) (photos
sourced from [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/)) and are used here purely to fill
a realistic-looking culling session for the docs.

The camera/EXIF and IPTC caption metadata visible in some shots (camera model,
byline, location, caption, …) is **fictional**, added purely to illustrate the
inspector and captioning views.

Cullimingo itself ships with no bundled photography.
